Project Description

This motion graphics project highlights dynamic visuals crafted to promote a high-end gift pack for a major product launch. Designed for digital and outdoor media, each animation showcases the product's unique selling points with sleek transitions, vibrant colors, and urban-inspired energy. The visuals were tailored to engage busy professionals and elevate the brand's premium identity across platforms.
